More about

I literally hate writing about myself, I never know what's worth mentioning or what people find interesting. Oh well ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

I'm a 20-something transmasc creator originally from California, but I've lived all over the US. Currently in Florida, but trying desperately to get out due to politics here. Growing up I spent most of my time playing Sonic or Pokemon, or one of the like 5 games I had for my GameCube, playing outside, or making arts and crafts with my mom. I got into anime at like 5 years old, and the chokehold Maximillion Pegasus had on my gender oh my god... Umm...

I work in tech, I won't mention where I work for the sake of my privacy, but I mostly just do configurations for systems my company uses. I have a professional portfolio site for work related stuff, but I really wanted my own personal space to be myself without the fear of a potential employer seeing and being able to tie to my identity. I started teaching myself how to code a few years ago through FreeCodeCamp tutorials on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After that, I took part in a bootcamp in hopes of getting a career in tech, it was one of the worst decisions and biggest wastes of time in my life, but it did get me a job.

I spend most of my time either doodling my faves or my OCs, I love character design and fashion, so designing characters is just everything to me. I like making new tech projects, when I can, I see this site being the biggest one in the near future, though. I also enjoy gaming with my partner and our friends, and spending time with our dog SunRay!
